# Understanding Huskies

A framework for thinking about Huskies — what they are, what they need, and what that asks of an owner.

Not a breed guide. Not a list of tips. A stepping stone for anyone living with one, thinking about getting one, or just trying to understand the breed beyond the stereotypes.

Work in progress. Built iteratively alongside community conversations.
